## Install

<Steps>
  <Step title="Install the extension">
    Install MemoryBase from the [Chrome Web Store](https://chromewebstore.google.com/detail/MemoryBase/bbdkfffldjdhpndnpcljnfccdhhacjma). Click **Add to Chrome** and confirm when prompted.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sign in">
    Click the MemoryBase icon in your toolbar and sign in with your email. You'll receive a 6-digit code.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start chatting">
    Visit [chatgpt.com](https://chatgpt.com) or [claude.ai](https://claude.ai). MemoryBase captures conversations automatically.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What happens after capture

1. **PII sanitized on-device** — identifiable data is scrubbed before leaving your browser.
2. **Embeddings generated locally** — semantic vectors are created in-browser for search.
3. **Synced to cloud** — the sanitized conversation is enriched with tags, topics, and a summary.
4. **Searchable everywhere** — available for context injection on any device.
