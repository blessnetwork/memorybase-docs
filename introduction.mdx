## What is Memorybase

Memorybase is a Chrome extension and cloud backend that **gives your AI conversations persistent, searchable memory**. It captures conversations from ChatGPT and Claude, extracts structured knowledge, and syncs everything across your devices.

No more re-explaining your preferences, project constraints, or prompt patterns. Your context is always with you.
