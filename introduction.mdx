## Why Memorybase

AI platforms have no memory between sessions. When a chat ends, the context you built — your preferred tone, your project constraints, your hard-won prompt patterns — disappears. Start a new conversation and you're explaining yourself from scratch, again.

Memorybase solves this by building a persistent, searchable memory layer that works across platforms and devices. Everything you've learned in past conversations is one click away in your next one.

## Key value props

**Automatic capture** — Memorybase observes supported AI platforms passively. You don't need to copy, paste, or remember to save anything. Conversations are captured as you have them.

**On-device PII sanitization** — Before any data leaves your browser, Memorybase scrubs personally identifiable information using a combination of regex patterns and named entity recognition. Your raw conversations never reach the cloud.

**Cross-device sync** — Your memory is stored in the cloud and available on every device where you've signed in. Switch from your laptop to your work desktop and your context comes with you.

**Semantic search** — Embeddings are generated on-device using a local model, with no API calls or external dependencies. Search finds relevant context by meaning, not just keyword matching.

**Server-side extraction** — Once your conversation is synced, the backend enriches it with tags, topics, and summaries generated by an LLM, making it easier to find and organize later.

## Supported AI platforms

Memorybase currently captures conversations from:

- **ChatGPT** (`chatgpt.com` and `chat.openai.com`)
- **Claude** (`claude.ai`)

Support for additional platforms is planned. On any unsupported site, you can still save content manually using the right-click context menu.

<Note>
  Platform adapters monitor their own health. If a site's DOM changes in a way that breaks automatic capture, Memorybase falls back to manual mode and notifies you in the extension popup.
</Note>
