---
title: "Projects"
description: "Group related conversations into projects to keep things organized."
---

A project groups related conversations around a theme -- a client, a feature, a research topic. MemoryBase uses these conversations to build a [context pack](/features/context-packs) you can share with any AI tool.

## Creating a project

Open the **Projects** tab, click **New project**, and give it a name and optional description.

## Adding content

- **Conversations** -- click **Add conversations** to link existing chats to the project
- **Notes** -- attach freehand text like decisions or requirements
- **URLs** -- link to external resources
- **Snippets** -- attach code blocks or configurations

## AI-suggested projects

MemoryBase can suggest projects based on your recent conversations. Open the **Projects** tab and click **Suggestions** to see them.
