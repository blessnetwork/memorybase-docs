---
title: "Projects"
description: "Group related conversations into projects to keep things organized."
---

A project groups related conversations around a theme -- a client, a feature, a research topic. MemoryBase uses these conversations to build a [context pack](/features/context-packs) you can share with any AI platform.

## AI-suggested projects

MemoryBase automatically suggests projects based on your recent conversations. You can also create a project yourself -- once you do, MemoryBase will suggest conversations that it thinks belong in that project.
