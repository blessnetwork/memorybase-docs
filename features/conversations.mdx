MemoryBase saves every conversation you have with ChatGPT, Claude, or Gemini. Each one is automatically tagged, summarized, and made searchable.

## What gets captured

For each conversation, MemoryBase automatically generates:

- A **summary** of what was discussed
- **Tags** and **topics** for easy discovery
- The full **message history**

You can also add your own tags, topics, and summaries.

## Organizing conversations

- **Add to projects** -- group related conversations together (see [Projects](/features/projects))
- **Archive** -- hide conversations you don't need right now without deleting them
