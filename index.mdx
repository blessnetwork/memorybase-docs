Memorybase is a Chrome extension paired with a cloud backend that gives your AI conversations a persistent, searchable memory. It passively captures conversations from AI platforms like ChatGPT and Claude, extracts structured knowledge server-side, and syncs everything to the cloud — so your context is always with you, no matter which device or AI tool you use.

## How Memorybase works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Install the extension">
    Download and load the Memorybase Chrome extension, then sign up with your email address.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start capturing">
    Visit ChatGPT or Claude and have a conversation. Memorybase captures it automatically in the background.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Organize with projects">
    Group related conversations into projects. Memorybase builds a context pack for each project that summarizes your knowledge.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Access everywhere">
    Your memory syncs to the cloud. Open a new chat on any device and inject relevant context with one click.
  </Step>
</Steps>
